I am a researcher focused on cryosphere, particularly **snow** and **permafrost processes**. This website hosts my work, blog, and updates.

---

My main research interests include:

-   High mountain permafrost processes,
-   Permafrost modelling,
-   Surface energy balance of snow, glacier and permafrost,
-   Watershed modelling, climate change, natural hazards,
-   Snow Processes
-   Hydro-meteorological monitoring

To understand these processes, I use computer models and other tools like remote sensing, geographic information systems (GIS), data science (R programming language) together with field trips.
