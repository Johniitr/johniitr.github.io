I am **John Mohd Wani**, a researcher specializing in **Cryosphere Science**. My work focuses on Alpine Hydrology, Mountain Permafrost, and Snow Modeling.

I utilize physically-based models to bridge the gap between field observations and theoretical understanding, aiming to reduce uncertainty in water resource predictions for high-altitude regions.

## Research Focus

### **1. Snow Dynamics**
Investigating energy-balance modeling of **snowmelt** in the Himalayas to understand diurnal and seasonal variations.

### **2. Modeling**
Applying catchment-scale forecasting tools (GEOframe, GEOtop) to simulate runoff in data-scarce mountain environments.

### **3. Remote Sensing**
Integrating multi-spectral satellite data (Sentinel-2, Landsat) with ground validation to monitor cryospheric changes over time.
