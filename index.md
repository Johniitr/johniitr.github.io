Welcome to my research website. I am **John Mohd Wani**, a researcher in Cryosphere. I study alpine hydrology, mountain permafrost, snow modeling, and use physically-based models to improve our understanding of mountain systems.


## Research Focus
* **Snow Dynamics:** Energy-balance modeling of snowm in the Himalayas.
* **Hydrological Modeling:** Using SWAT and HEC-HMS for catchment-scale forecasting.
* **Remote Sensing:** Integrating satellite data (Sentinel/Landsat) with ground validation.
