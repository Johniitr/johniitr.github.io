Welcome to my research website. I am **John Mohd Wani**, a researcher in Cryospher. I study the intersection of **Alpine Hydrology**, **Snow Modeling**, and **Remote Sensing**.

My research aims to reduce uncertainty in hydrological predictions for high-altitude regions, bridging the gap between field observations and physically-based models.

## Research Focus
* **Snow Dynamics:** Energy-balance modeling of snowmelt in the Himalayas.
* **Hydrological Modeling:** Using SWAT and HEC-HMS for catchment-scale forecasting.
* **Remote Sensing:** Integrating satellite data (Sentinel/Landsat) with ground validation.
